Join video meetings instantly. No downloads, no sign-ups required.

Let's learn how to join a meeting →

Step 1: Room Name

Enter the room name that the meeting organizer gave you. For example: "design-review" or "team-sync"

Room names are shared and can't be private.

Step 2: Your Display Name

Enter your name so others can see who you are. This appears next to your video and in the participants list.

⭐ Important for Room Creators:

If you created this room, enter the exact SAME display name you used when creating it. This makes you the admin so you can remove unwanted participants.

Step 3: Join Room

Click "Join Room" to connect. Our system will ask for camera and microphone permissions (optional, you can skip them).

You can adjust audio/video settings once you're in the room.

Admin Controls

If you're the room creator and joined with your creation name, you'll see a "ADMIN" badge and "Remove" buttons next to other participants.

🛡️ What You Can Do as Admin:

• Remove unwanted participants by clicking "Remove" next to their name
• Control who participates in your meeting
• Ensure a secure and professional environment
